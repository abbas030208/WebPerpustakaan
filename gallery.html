Featured Books

BUMI

Bumi is the first novel in the Bumi series, taking readers into a fantasy world full of adventure. The story follows Raib, a teenager with the extraordinary ability to disappear. Together with her friends Seli and Ali, they explore a parallel world full of mysteries. This series is popular among young readers for its thrilling and imaginative plot.

DILAN

Dilan: Dia adalah Dilanku Tahun 1990 is a teenage novel by Pidi Baiq that tells the love story between Milea and Dilan, a unique and charismatic young man from Bandung in 1990. Set in a high school in Bandung, the novel features Dilan's funny and sweet antics and his unique way of approaching Milea.

Read More
HUJAN

Hujan is a novel by Tere Liye set in a post-apocalyptic future where the Earth has undergone climate-altering disasters. The story follows Lail, a girl struggling to recover from trauma caused by a major disaster, accompanied by her loyal friend Esok. Central themes include love, friendship, loss, and human resilience.

Read More
LASKAR PELANGI

Laskar Pelangi by Andrea Hirata is one of Indonesia's best-selling novels, inspired by the author's childhood in Belitung. It tells the story of eleven children from poor families attending a nearly-closed Muhammadiyah elementary school. Known as Laskar Pelangi, they show great spirit in learning and changing their fate.

SURGA YANG TAK DIRINDUKAN

Surga yang Tak Dirindukan by Asma Nadia explores love, family, and sacrifice in facing life's tests. The story centers on Pras, happily married to Arini, until their happiness is tested when Pras decides to marry Mei Rose, a severely depressed woman needing help.

ANCIKA

Ancika: Dia yang Bersamaku Tahun 1995 by Pidi Baiq continues the popular Dilan series. It explores Dilan's life after his relationship with Milea ends, focusing on his new relationship with Ancika, a strong-willed girl who brings new colors to Dilan's life.
